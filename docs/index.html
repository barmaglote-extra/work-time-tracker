About Work Day Tracker

Work Day Tracker is a powerful yet intuitive work hour tracking application designed to help you manage your workday efficiently. Built with C++ and Qt6, this application helps you monitor your work time, track breaks, and visualize your productivity.


Key Features

Time Tracking

Start, pause, resume, and stop your work timer to accurately track your work hours.

Workday Management

Set daily work hour goals and break requirements to maintain a healthy work-life balance.

Visual Feedback

Real-time display of elapsed time and remaining work time for better time awareness.

Finish Time Estimation

Predicts when your workday will end based on current progress and configured goals.

Statistics & Analytics

View your work patterns and productivity metrics with interactive charts showing daily work durations.

System Tray Integration

Minimize to system tray and receive notifications without interrupting your workflow.
